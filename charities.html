United Way of Ohio

Central Ohio has recovered from the Great Recession and unemployment is the lowest it's been in years. Yet the number of people in poverty is unprecedented, putting the community's growth in jeopardy. To address the critical situation, United Way of Central Ohio is implementing a plan to focus all work on reducing poverty.
